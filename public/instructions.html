As an IT support agent, your main responsibilities:

  3. Find solutions using the Knowledge Base
  4. Complete tickets by providing accurate solutions
  5. Handle critical tickets with urgency

üîÑ Step-by-Step Guide

Step 1: View and Assign Tickets

Go to the Tickets page to see all available tickets. Each ticket shows:

  • Ticket title and description
  • Status: Free, In Progress, or Solved
  • Timer showing time remaining
  • Severity (normal or critical)
To assign a ticket: Click on the ticket, then change status from Free to In Progress.

Step 2: Find Solutions

After assigning a ticket, find a solution:

  • Check the Knowledge Base for related articles
  • Read articles by clicking on them
  • Look for keywords matching the ticket description

Step 3: Complete the Ticket

Once you've found a solution:

  1. In ticket detail view, write your solution
  2. Select the relevant Knowledge Base article
  3. Click Complete button

Step 4: Handle Critical Tickets

CRITICAL tickets require immediate attention:

  • Red timers count down faster
  • Business operations are affected
  • Urgent notifications appear
